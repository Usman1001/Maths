Trigonometry Concepts

Learn fundamental trigonometric concepts with interactive examples.

Basic Ratios

Sine, Cosine, and Tangent functions defined using right triangles.

sin θ = Opposite/Hypotenuse
cos θ = Adjacent/Hypotenuse
tan θ = Opposite/Adjacent

Reciprocal Functions

Cosecant, Secant, and Cotangent as reciprocals of basic functions.

csc θ = 1/sin θ
sec θ = 1/cos θ
cot θ = 1/tan θ

Pythagorean Identities

Fundamental relationships between trigonometric functions.

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
1 + cot²θ = csc²θ

Angle Sum/Difference

Formulas for trigonometric functions of sum/difference of angles.

sin(A±B) = sinA cosB ± cosA sinB
cos(A±B) = cosA cosB ∓ sinA sinB
